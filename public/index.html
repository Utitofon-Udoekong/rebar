01 How it works
STEP 01
Read
carefully
Before writing a line of code, understand what the client actually needs — not just what they said. Extract the explicit ask, the implied constraints, and what a genuinely good version looks like.
STEP 02
Pick the
right tool
Stack is determined by the job, not by habit. Simple tool? Single HTML file — opens instantly. Complex app? Nuxt 4 or Next.js. Game? Canvas. The best output wins over the familiar one.
STEP 03
Build it
properly
No TODOs. No placeholder content. No stub functions. Realistic data. Error states handled. Responsive layout. Every interactive element does exactly what it looks like it does.
STEP 04
Add one
more thing
Every job gets one bonus enhancement — functional, visible, genuinely useful. Not scope creep. The one thing that makes the client feel like they got more than they paid for.
